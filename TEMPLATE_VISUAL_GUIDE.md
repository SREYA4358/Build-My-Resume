## Detailed Template Descriptions

### 📘 Template 1: Clean Classic
**Visual Identity**: Traditional, timeless, professional

**Key Features**:
- Serif font (Georgia) for classic look
- Navy blue color scheme (#1e40af)
- Gradient header background (light blue to white)
- 3px solid border under header
- Bold section headers with underlines
- Light blue skill badges with borders

**When to Use**:
- Executive positions
- Senior management roles
- Traditional industries (finance, law, consulting)
- When you want to appear established and experienced

---

### 🌊 Template 2: Modern
**Visual Identity**: Contemporary, fresh, dynamic

**Key Features**:
- Modern sans-serif font (Trebuchet MS)
- Teal accent color (#14b8a6)
- 6px left border for sidebar effect
- Colored section backgrounds
- Rounded skill badges
- Gradient header background

**When to Use**:
- Creative professionals
- Marketing and design roles
- Startups and tech companies
- When you want to appear innovative and forward-thinking

---

### 📊 Template 3: Professional
**Visual Identity**: Corporate, structured, organized

**Key Features**:
- Clean Arial font
- Gray color scheme (#374151)
- Boxed sections with borders
- Structured layout with clear separation
- White headers on gray backgrounds
- Bordered skill badges

**When to Use**:
- Corporate positions
- Technical roles (engineering, IT)
- Government or institutional jobs
- When you want to appear organized and detail-oriented

---

### 🎨 Template 4: Creative
**Visual Identity**: Artistic, bold, eye-catching

**Key Features**:
- Modern Poppins font
- Purple to pink gradient (#8b5cf6 to #ec4899)
- Gradient header with white text
- Rounded section headers
- Gradient skill badges
- Light purple background

**When to Use**:
- Design positions (UI/UX, graphic design)
- Creative industries (advertising, media)
- Portfolio-based roles
- When you want to stand out and show creativity

---

### ✨ Template 5: Minimal
**Visual Identity**: Clean, modern, sophisticated

**Key Features**:
- Ultra-clean Inter font
- Subtle blue accents (#3b82f6)
- Lots of whitespace
- Thin borders
- Uppercase section headers
- Outlined skill badges
- Light gray text for content

**When to Use**:
- Recent graduates
- Entry-level positions
- Tech startups
- When you want to appear modern and focused on content

---

### 🚀 Template 6: One-Page Compact
**Visual Identity**: Efficient, concise, information-dense

**Key Features**:
- Compact Roboto font (0.85rem)
- Green color scheme (#10b981)
- Reduced spacing throughout
- Gradient header
- Smaller skill badges
- Condensed line height (1.5)

**When to Use**:
- Job fairs
- Quick applications
- When space is limited
- Networking events
- When you need everything on one page

## Typography Hierarchy

### Template 1 (Classic)
- H1: Georgia, 1.75rem, Navy
- H2: Georgia, 1rem, Navy, Bold, Underlined
- Body: Georgia, 0.875rem, Dark blue

### Template 2 (Modern)
- H1: Trebuchet MS, 1.75rem, Black
- H2: Trebuchet MS, 1rem, Teal, Background
- Body: Trebuchet MS, 0.875rem, Black

### Template 3 (Professional)
- H1: Arial, 1.75rem, Dark gray
- H2: Arial, 1rem, Dark gray, Bold, Left border
- Body: Arial, 0.875rem, Dark gray

### Template 4 (Creative)
- H1: Poppins, 1.75rem, White (on gradient)
- H2: Poppins, 1rem, White, Gradient background, Rounded
- Body: Poppins, 0.875rem, Purple

## Layout Differences

### Single Column (1, 3, 5, 6)
- Full-width content
- Sections stacked vertically
- Clear hierarchy

### Sidebar Effect (2)
- Left border creates visual sidebar
- Colored section backgrounds
- Asymmetric feel

### Boxed Sections (3)
- Each section in its own box
- Clear separation
- Structured appearance

### Artistic (4)
- Gradient elements
- Rounded corners
- Visual interest

## Skill Badge Styles

1. **Classic**: Filled light blue with border
2. **Modern**: Filled teal, rounded
3. **Professional**: Gray with border
4. **Creative**: Gradient fill, rounded
5. **Minimal**: Outlined only, no fill
6. **Compact**: Small filled green

## Quick Selection Guide

**Choose Template 1 if**: You want traditional, professional, executive look
**Choose Template 2 if**: You want modern, creative, dynamic feel
**Choose Template 3 if**: You want corporate, structured, organized appearance
**Choose Template 4 if**: You want artistic, bold, eye-catching design
**Choose Template 5 if**: You want minimal, clean, content-focused layout
**Choose Template 6 if**: You need compact, one-page, efficient format
